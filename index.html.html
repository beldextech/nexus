Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Beldex

What is Beldex (BDX)?
Beldex is a privacy-focused blockchain ecosystem offering a suite of decentralized applications including BChat, BelNet VPN and Beldex Wallet. It uses advanced cryptographic technology to ensure complete anonymity for users and their transactions.
How can I buy BDX?
BDX is available on trusted exchanges like KoinBX and CoinDCX. You can deposit INR, buy BDX and store it in your Beldex Wallet. Visit our Exchange Partners page for direct links to each platform.
What are the staking plans?
We offer three plans — Regular (2% monthly), Combo (3% + 400% fixed bonus) and Elite (4.75–5.5% monthly). All plans run for 5 years with guaranteed returns. Visit the Plans page for detailed tables.
Is Beldex safe and secure?
Yes. Beldex runs on a Proof-of-Stake blockchain with a masternode network, smart contract audits and a bug bounty program. In 2023, DWF Labs invested $25M, validating the ecosystem's credibility and security.
What is BelNet and BChat?
BelNet is Beldex's decentralized VPN that routes your internet traffic anonymously. BChat is a private, end-to-end encrypted messaging app built on the Beldex blockchain — no phone number required.
How do I contact support?
Our team is available 24×7. You can reach us via the Contact page, email us at support@beldex.io, or use the WhatsApp and Telegram buttons on this page for instant assistance.

Who We Are

About Beldex

A privacy-focused blockchain ecosystem offering decentralized applications for secure and anonymous online interactions. Empowering individuals with true digital freedom.

🚀 Our Mission

To provide a comprehensive environment where individuals can exchange data and perform transactions with true confidentiality. We focus on individual safety and privacy in the digital age, addressing shortcomings of traditional centralized platforms.

🌍 Our Vision

To address widespread privacy leakage in Web3 by offering a full suite of privacy applications. Operating on our own decentralized blockchain with Proof-of-Stake consensus and a masternode network ensuring security, scalability and anonymity.

⭐ Why Choose Beldex

Beldex (BDX) is chosen for its comprehensive focus on user digital privacy and anonymity across both financial transactions and everyday online activities. A complete, decentralized ecosystem that is a secure alternative to surveillance-heavy platforms.
